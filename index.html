Game Instructions

The game is a simple one, you must guide Gendry through Westeros's great houses. He must obtain their support, loyalty
... and gold.

Use each house's sigil to guide you through the game, always moving to the left. Be wary of King's Landing for the queen must not find out - or she'll have his head!


While visiting each house, you must make desicions the lords and ladies will find favorable. This will gain you points. When you've reached more than 400 points - you are ready to move against the Queen. Be wary of liars, everyone has their own agenda.


Leaderboard will contain points obtained from Barathon pledged houses, they have given their support! For the true King.
